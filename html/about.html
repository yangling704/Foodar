How it Works!



REGISTER FOR AN ACCOUNT


The first thin you need to do is register for an account.After Registering you will be able to submit requests for pickup and have your food deliver to your door!





ORDER YOUR FOOD


Place an order for food from a local restaurant and the system will automatically find the nearest driver at the best cost.





CONFIRM YOUR REQUEST

Before any request is submitted, you have the chance to review it and make any changes or cancellation.
